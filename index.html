Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple recipie has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375˚ F (190˚ C)
  2. In a bowl, miix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples int the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
